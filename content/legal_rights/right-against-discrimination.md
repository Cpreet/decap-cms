    body: "Articles 14 and 15 of the Constitution prohibit discrimination on grounds of religion, caste, race, sex, or place of birth. Article 21 protects the right to life and dignity. These principles can be invoked against housing exclusion."
    legal_ref: "Constitution of India — Articles 14, 15, 21 (Full Text)"

    body: "Discrimination based on caste and religion is strictly prohibited. If you are denied accommodation solely because of your religion or caste, this Act provides legal remedies."
    legal_ref: "Protection of Civil Rights Act, 1955 — Full Text (PDF)"
    source_url: "https://legislative.gov.in/sites/default/files/A1955-22.pdf"

    body: "For Dalit renters facing caste-based humiliation, denial of access, or public insult — this is a cognisable offence. Police are legally required to file an FIR. Document every incident carefully."
    legal_ref: "SC/ST Act, 1989 — Full Text (PDF)"
