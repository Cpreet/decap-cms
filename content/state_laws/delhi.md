excerpt: "Delhi's 1958 Act protects tenants in covered premises: eviction requires court order on specific grounds (non-payment, misuse, landlord's need). No arbitrary rent hikes — standard rent fixed by Controller. Water/electricity cannot be cut during disputes. Tenants must deposit rent monthly with Controller if disputed. No statutory deposit cap mentioned. Written notice precedes legal eviction process. Applies mainly to pre-1988 tenancies below certain rent thresholds. Modern high-rent leases follow contract law."
