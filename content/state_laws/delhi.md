excerpt: "Governed by the Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 and Delhi Rent Act, 1995. No MTA adoption yet. Tenants cannot be evicted without a court order, and water and electricity cannot be cut off during disputes. No legal deposit cap exists — always negotiate the amount in writing. Written notice is mandatory before eviction. Unfair rent increases can be challenged through the Rent Controller."
