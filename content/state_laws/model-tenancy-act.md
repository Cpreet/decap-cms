excerpt: "The Model Tenancy Act proposes that states adopt standardised rental rules: security deposits capped at 2 months' rent (residential), mandatory written agreements registered with the Rent Authority, eviction permitted only under certain grounds, and a 3-tier dispute system (Rent Authority → Rent Court → Tribunal). The MTA was approved by the Union Cabinet on June 2, 2021 for adoption by states and union territories and seeks to: (i) establish a speedy adjudication mechanism for dispute resolution, (ii) regulate renting of premises, and (iii) protect interests of landlords and tenants."
