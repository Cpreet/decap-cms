excerpt: "Applies to Calcutta, Howrah, and specified municipal areas. Eviction requires Controller order on grounds like 3 months' rent arrears or landlord's reasonable need for rebuilding. Fair rent fixed by Controller — no excess charges or premiums allowed without permission. Essential services must continue. Tenants cannot sublet without written consent. Written notice required before proceedings. Disputes go to Controller with Civil Court appeal. No statutory security deposit cap specified. Landlords must maintain premises in tenantable condition. Strong protection against arbitrary eviction and rent exploitation."
